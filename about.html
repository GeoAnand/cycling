 Who We Are

Started in the year 2012, Cycling Yogis is a group for people who have in common, passion towards cycling and respect for our heritage. Every year we organize events to mark the occasion of Republic Day (January), World Heritage Day (April) and Madras Day (August). We also publish books on numerous trails in the city and curate Vintage Bicycles Exhibition, the Chennai based group is now adding culinary heritage to its agenda
