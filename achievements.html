 Vintage Bicycle Exhibition

Since the year 2018 Vintage Bicycles Exhibition have been held on the occasion of Madras Week Celebration. Vintage brands on display include Raleigh, Humber, Rudge, Phillips, BSA , Hercules, Robinhood, Paratrooper Bicycles of Second World War period, Pierce Bicycle of early 1900's and Road Bikes. Also in the exhibits are varieties of Vintage Bicycle Lamps (Oil, Kerosene, Candle Models), Dynamos, Saddle Bags, Tool Kits, Bicycle Licenses, Manuals, Bells and Tricycles.
